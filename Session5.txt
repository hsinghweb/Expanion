In this session, aspirant will have a 1-1 mock interview with expert and once the interview is complete, expert willwill provide his feedback and share details on preparing for the actual interviews in a product based company.
Average duration of 14 days
Session Deliverables

    The project will be taken to closure, it will be deployed in live production or the app will be uploaded to app store
    Expert conducts the Mock interview.
    Shares the feedback on where the aspirant needs to improve before real interview.
